PHOTOGRAPHY

by: Group 7

"Photography is a way of feeling, of touching, of loving. What you have caught on film is captured forever ... It remembers little things, long after you have forgotten everything." -Aaron Siskind

Candid - un-posed and unplanned photo.

Posed- communication between photographer and the subject about the body position & expression.

Formal - communication between photographer and the subject regarding clothing and mood.

Couple-interpretation between two people.

Small Group - interpretation between members of a group.

Enviromental - shows the subject(s) in their own territory.

Sporting- shows the subject with their uniform , equipment in their sporting environment.

Portrait Tips

Choosing the Right Background , Focus on the Eyes , Lighting and more lighting , Change the Format Framing , Alter your Perspective , Play with focus , Movement , Get serious also Reflection & Shadow.
